N°1 Robotics School

Robotics education is becoming increasingly commonplace in schools. This is largely due to the fact that students in 12 grade will graduate into a workforce that’s rife with technology, in an era where robots will become widely used in our everyday lives. Even if these kids choose a career other than coding or robotics, learning robotics teaches them many important skills such as analytical thinking, programming, teamwork, collaborative thinking, innovation and more. It can be easily incorporated into STEM education, which is becoming more popular in schools worldwide.

We are a team of Software Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Mechanical Engineers are trying provide robotics education nationally to develop the quality, skills and motivate the students to be a great programmer, roboticist as well as leadership in particular area.
